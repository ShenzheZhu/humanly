If anyone here has finished an early draft at a young age, I'd love to hear how you approached revising it without getting discouraged. Did you set it aside for a while? Did you rewrite from scratch? I'm proud of the milestone, but I'm also a little overwhelmed by how much work clearly still lies ahead. Any advice on what to focus on first would genuinely mean a lot.
