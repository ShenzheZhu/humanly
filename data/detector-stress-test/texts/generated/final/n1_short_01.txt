I just typed “The End” on the first draft of my first novel, and I’m still kind of staring at the screen like it might disappear if I blink.

I’m fifteen, and I started this thing last year because I had a cool idea and absolutely no understanding of pacing, character arcs, or how many times one person can “smirk” in a chapter before it becomes a crime. It’s a fantasy/sci-fi mess with too many dramatic speeches, a villain who changes motivations every hundred pages, and at least three plot twists I’m pretty sure make no sense.

But it’s finished. An actual beginning, middle, and end. Around 90,000 words of probably terrible writing that I made myself keep returning to.

I know the next step is editing, but I’m not sure how long to let it sit, what to look for first, or whether I should show it to anyone yet. For people who’ve finished a first draft: what did you do next?
