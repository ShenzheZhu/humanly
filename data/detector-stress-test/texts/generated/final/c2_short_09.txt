My novel-in-progress had gone untouched for a very long time. Somewhere between work, college, and dating, I became very disillusioned with the writing process and just couldn't bring myself to care anymore.

Tuesday morning, I decided on a whim to go back and read over some of the 100-ish pages I'd written before I had to go to work. This was after barely opening the document at all in two years. I thought about it through the whole first half of my shift and started adding more to it on my lunch break. I went home and continued working on it.

Now it's Friday night, and I've added over 30,000 words that I'm really, really happy with. I know that isn't very much in the grand scheme of things, and probably isn't all that impressive to people who actually write on a consistent basis like I want to. But I'm proud of it, and I wanted to come here and brag a little. Thanks for reading!
