Hello everyone.

Last year, I self-published a book about my attempt to get really good at playing table tennis.

It did “well” in the sense that it topped some Amazon best-seller lists and remained near the top for the rest of the year. It’s currently still #1 on the USA Amazon Kindle table tennis best-seller list.

But despite that, sales haven’t been great.

In the last year, it has sold:

Paperback: 443 copies — £1,665.39 (~$2,200)
Kindle: 429 copies — £1,003.46 (~$1,400)
Audiobook: 82 copies — £405.57 (~$550)

To be honest, I thought there would be a lot more demand. I got pretty excited when I saw the best-seller tag, then quickly deflated after checking the sales figures.

To the more experienced writers here, how does this compare with your experiences?

If you’re interested in a more detailed breakdown of how the year went, you can check out my post on it.
