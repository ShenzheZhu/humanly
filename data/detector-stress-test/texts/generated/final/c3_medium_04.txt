Psychology is the science that studies the mind and behavior in interaction with context. Although these concepts may sound familiar, they are notably difficult to define. Philosophers and scientists have tried for hundreds of years to delimit the entities we refer to when we use them, without reaching a definitive agreement. For that reason, one of the most obvious and natural questions when we become interested in this science is also one of the most difficult to answer: “What does psychology study?” In this learning project we will try to answer that question. We will not do so by proposing a definition, but by taking a high-level tour through this field of study. In this way we hope to give a general idea of its scope and to familiarize the reader with the basic concepts needed to go deeper into other topics of interest related to the “human mind.”
