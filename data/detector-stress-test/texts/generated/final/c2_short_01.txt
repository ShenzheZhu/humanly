I’ve always loved writing and reading. In fact, I wrote my first short story when I was four years old. It was about a spaceman who goes out into space and “thought” all the animals. (Forgive me: four-year-old me actually knew how to spell “fought,” but he thought “thoughting” animals was cooler.) Anyway, today I finished the first draft of my first book (384 pages!!!). It is a sci-fi dystopian novel that can be described as what would happen if all the clichés from every piece of YA fiction gathered in a room, shat in a bucket, and then sprinkled it with magical purple-prose powder that made the shit shittier than it already was. Nonetheless, I am still flying in a state of pure bliss. AMA.
