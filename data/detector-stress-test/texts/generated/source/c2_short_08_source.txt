After reading a bunch of threads on /r/FanTheories I thought about the plot for the novel I'm writing and started considering if there were any scenarios that were ambiguous enough to allow such a thing.
I started thinking along the lines of "what if this was really this?" or "what if this character wasn't trying to do this but actually this?" and realised there was actually room for a huge underlying conspiracy that alters the dynamic of the story drastically while keeping all of the plot details completely in-tact. I worried my plot was too linear but now I'm finding so much room for depth where I'd previously felt there was none.
Maybe it's just the coffee or how late it is here but this felt like such a eureka moment and I thought I'd share it. It probably won't work for every plot, but the way this one just fell into place was so wonderfully satisfying, and even a bit mind-blowing, so I'd recommend it to anyone who isn't 100% sure of their plot.
Many an excited scribble tonight.
Edit: This got more attention than I expected! I hope the people who've read this see as much joy from the idea as I did.
