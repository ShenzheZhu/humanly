My novel-in-progress has gone untouched for a very long time. Somewhere between work, college, and dating, I became very dissilusioned with the writing process and just couldn't bring myself to care anymore.
Teusday morning, I decided on a whim to go back and read over some of the 100-ish pages I'd written before I had to go to work. This was after barely opening the document at all in two years. I thought about it the whole first half of my shift, and starting adding more to it on my lunch break. I went home and continued working on.
Now it's Friday night, and I've added over 30,000 words that I'm really, really happy with. I know that isn't really very much in the grand scheme of things, and probably isn't all that impressive to people who actually do write on a consistent basis like I want to. But I was proud of it, and I wanted to come here and brag a little. Thanks for reading!
