I'm still a little stunned, but I received an offer today and I wanted to share it somewhere people would understand. For years this felt like a far-off dream, the kind of thing that happens to other writers. I'd collected enough rejections to wallpaper a room, and I'd started to make peace with the idea that maybe it just wasn't going to happen for me. Then the email arrived, and I had to read it three times to be sure I wasn't misunderstanding. I don't say this to brag, because I know how much luck and timing are involved alongside the work. Mostly I want anyone still in the querying trenches to know that persistence genuinely matters, and that a long string of "no" doesn't predict the next answer. I'm trying to stay grounded, because I know the real work is just beginning. But today I'm letting myself feel the joy. Thank you all for the encouragement along the way.
